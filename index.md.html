**lukas paul achatius galke** postdoc, max planck institute for psycholinguistics Hi there, I'm Lukas. My main research interest is lifelong machine communication. At the intersection of text and graph representation learning, I am exploring how we can build machine learning models that are efficient, multimodal, can be continually updated.
